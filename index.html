Little reminders for this month

1. Study in short, focused blocks — 45 minutes on, 10 minutes off works better than marathon sessions.
2. Sleep is not optional right now. Your brain needs it to actually remember what you studied.
3. Revise past papers, not just notes — knowing the format matters as much as knowing the content.
4. Drink water, eat properly, and take real breaks. Your body carries you through this too.
5. One bad practice test doesn't mean anything about the real exam. Keep going.
6. You don't have to feel ready every day. Some days just showing up to study is enough.
